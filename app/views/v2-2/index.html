Eligibility

You may be able to get help with the cost of visiting a close relative or partner in prison if you’re aged over 16, are on certain benefits or have a low income.

Relationship

You may be eligible if you are the prisoner’s:

  • close relative, partner, only visitor, or
  • escorting a child, young person or adult
More information

Benefits

You may qualify if you are receiving:

  • Income Support, Jobseeker’s Allowance, Employment and Support Allowance, Universal Credit, Working Tax Credits, Child Tax Credit, Pension Credit, or
  • Have a Health Certificate (HC2 or HC3)
More information

First time applicant?

You’ll need to have:

  • Your National Insurance number
  • Prisoner’s number and name of the Prison

You may also need to send paper documents to us electronically. We will explain how to do this during your application.
